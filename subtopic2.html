URL The Impact of iPhones on Politics: Shaping Public Opinion, Citizen Journalism, and the Future of Voting
Site Author Politics Insider
Abstract This article explores how iPhones have impacted politics while also emphasizing on how they have shaped public conversation, disseminated political material, and amplified citizen voices. iPhones' rapid access to social media, news, and live-streaming features have enabled people to record occurrences and subvert conventional wisdom. Additionally, the study examines how mobile technology is raising political consciousness and engagement, especially among younger generations. Lastly, it examines how advancements like secure digital ballots and mobile voter registration might revolutionize voting in the future.


URL Digital Technologies and the Future of Journalism
Site Author
Abstract The article "Digital Technologies and the Future of Journalism" from the Center for Innovation & Sustainability in Local Media highlights the quick transition from traditional tools used in media production to digital innovations and how such technologies are causing a shift in journalistic practices. Compared to the previous article that concentrated on the effects of iPhones on political participation and citizen journalism. this one has a more comprehensive examination of how digital innovations are changing the media environment. The need for journalists to adjust to these developments in order to continue collecting and disseminating news effectively is emphasized.
