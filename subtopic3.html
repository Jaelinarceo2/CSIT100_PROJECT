Softwares Used in Media Production

URL The Rise of Video Chats: How Virtual Conversations Are Shaping Digital Journalism
Site Author Jennifer Ross
Abstract This particular article explores the growing role of video communication platforms like Zoom and Google Meet in modern newsrooms. It also how virtual interviews and remote reporting have become standard tools for journalists, expanding access to sources and enabling real-time storytelling across distances. The article also examines how these digital interactions are reshaping audience expectations and redefining what it means to report in the digital age.


URL Why a Newspaper Replaced Its CMS with Google Docs and WordPress
Site Author O'Reilly Media
Abstract This article discusses why a local newspaper decided to replace its traditional content management system with a simpler workflow using Google Docs and WordPress. It then goes into depth about how the change improved collaboration among journalists, and reduced technical barriers. This article differs from the previous because while “The Rise of Video Chats” focuses on how virtual communication tools are shaping journalistic interviews and reporting methods, this article centers on backend newsroom operations and workflow efficiency. Instead of examining how news is gathered or presented to audiences, it highlights how stories are written, edited, and published behind the scenes.
