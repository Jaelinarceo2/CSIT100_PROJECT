Early Technology in Journalism

URL Tech and Evolution of News
Site Author CHM Editorial
Abstract The site explores how technological advancements have continually reshaped journalism and media regulation. It traces the historical shifts in news distribution, audience interaction, and business models while highlighting how digital tools and platforms are transforming modern journalism. It also examines challenges like misinformation, media consolidation, and the role of AI.


URL A Brief Look at the History of Broadcast Journalism
Site Author NYFA
Abstract This site explains how Edward R. Murrow transformed broadcast journalism. This site differs from the first one because it centers on the development of broadcast journalism, covering historical turning points in radio and television news, and how broadcast storytelling evolved over time. It also includes storytelling formats and well known broadcasters in the media.
